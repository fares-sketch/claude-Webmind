# 03 - PROMPTS SORA (OPENAI) - MODÈLE TURISMO

*Ces prompts sont optimisés pour la compréhension spatiale et temporelle de Sora. Ils intègrent la nouvelle double promesse : Frisson de la location vs Rentabilité de l'investissement.*

## Séquence 1 : Le Hook (L'Émotion & L'Investissement)
**Prompt :** > "Cinematic masterpiece, vertical 9:16 aspect ratio. A breathtaking rear-angle shot of a pristine white Porsche GT3 RS with its massive aero wing, bathed in dramatic, moody studio lighting. The environment is a minimalist, pure black void. Perfectly smooth, slow optical zoom towards the rear taillight. The car feels aggressive yet elegant, representing the pinnacle of automotive investment. Flawless reflections on the white paint. 8k resolution, photorealistic, high-end commercial style."

## Séquence 2 : L'Offre Locataire (L'Accès)
**Prompt :** > "First-person POV cinematic shot, vertical 9:16. The camera simulates someone sitting in the driver's seat of an ultra-luxury sports car. The hands gently grip a premium carbon-fiber and alcantara steering wheel. Through the windshield, a sleek, blurred modern architectural environment is visible. The lighting is soft and golden, conveying the extreme thrill and exclusivity of driving an inaccessible supercar for the weekend. Highly realistic, deep depth of field, 8k."

## Séquence 3 : L'Offre Propriétaire (Le Rendement)

The aesthetic screams high-net-worth wealth management and absolute security. Soft background bokeh, photorealism, slow camera push-in."

## Séquence 4 : L'Excellence du Service (L'Entretien)

The space is pristine, sterile, and inspires absolute trust for car owners. Slow tracking shot backward. 8k, photoreal."

## Séquence 5 : End Frame (Branding)
