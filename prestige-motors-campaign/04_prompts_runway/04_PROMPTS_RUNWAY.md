# 04 - PROMPTS RUNWAY (GEN-3) / HIGGSFIELD - MODÈLE TURISMO (DEEP PROMPTING)

*Optimisé pour l'Image-to-Video (I2V) afin de garantir la constance du véhicule (ex: la Porsche GT3 RS) et des interfaces. Les mouvements prescrits sont pensés pour tirer la quintessence de la lumière et des micro-textures.*

## 🚨 Stratégie "I2V" (Recommandée pour Gen-3 & Higgsfield)
1. Utilisez les images validées (Porsche GT3, Dashboard UI) comme "Image Initiale".
2. Copiez l'un des "Deep Prompts" ci-dessous qui frôlent les 1000 caractères, conçus pour forcer les modèles à injecter un maximum de détails architecturaux, de matériaux premium et de réalisme physique.

## Séquence 1 : Le Hook (L'Émotion & Investissement)
**Image cible :** La photo de la Porsche GT3 RS blanche de dos.
**Prompt (960 char) :** > "Cinematic masterpiece, breathtaking ultra-high fidelity photorealistic reveal of a pristine white Porsche GT3 RS. The camera focuses on the aggressive, aerodynamic rear wing and the flawless, glossy white paint reflecting pure perfection. The environment is an anechoic, pure black void—a hyper-minimalist brutalist luxury studio designed for the elite. Above, an exquisite, thin architectural softbox LED strip emits a dramatic, cold white light that slowly travels across the vehicle's curves, casting sharp, beautiful chiaroscuro shadows across the intricate carbon-fiber diffuser elements. The atmosphere is deeply intense, silent, and powerfully institutional, perfectly capturing the duality of pure driving emotion and massive financial investment value. Shot on 35mm anamorphic glass, shallow depth of field, incredibly crisp textural details on the metallic surfaces. Absolutely no people, no UI text, pure unadulterated automotive supremacy. Extremely slow, majestic push-in."
*(Réglage : Zoom In - Slow)*
